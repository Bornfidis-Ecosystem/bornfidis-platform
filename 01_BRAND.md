# BORNFIDIS BRAND KIT

## Brand Identity
**Name:** Bornfidis  
**Tagline:** Regenerating Land, People & Enterprise  
**Sub-brands:** Bornfidis Provisions (Food), Bornfidis Sportswear (Clothing)

## Color Palette
- **Primary:** Forest Green `#2C5F2D`
- **Secondary:** Warm Gold `#D4AF37`
- **Accent:** Earth Brown `#8B7355`
- **Neutral Light:** Cream `#F8F6F3`
- **Neutral Dark:** Charcoal `#424242`

## Typography
**Primary Font:** Montserrat (headings, nav, buttons)  
**Secondary Font:** Lora (body text, quotes, elegant touch)  
**Fallbacks:** System fonts (sans-serif for Montserrat, serif for Lora)

## Logo
- **Primary:** Wordmark "BORNFIDIS" in Montserrat Bold, forest green
- **Icon (if using):** Simple circular emblem with leaf or mountain motif
- **Formats needed:** SVG (primary), PNG (fallback)
- **Usage:** Logo always on cream or white backgrounds, never on busy images

## Voice & Tone

### DO:
- Professional but warm
- Grounded and specific
- Faith-anchored without being preachy
- Confident, not hype-driven
- Use active voice: "We build" not "We are building"

### DON'T:
- Startup jargon ("disruptive," "revolutionizing")
- Excessive adjectives
- Generic charity language ("helping the less fortunate")
- Corporate speak
- Over-promise scale or timelines

### Example Copy:
**Good:** "We serve premium farm-to-table meals in Vermont's Okemo Valley, connecting local farms to discerning diners."

**Bad:** "We're disrupting the food system by leveraging innovative partnerships to revolutionize farm-to-table experiences."

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts
- Natural textures (linen, canvas)
- High-quality food photography (vibrant, honest, not overstyled)
- Minimal use of icons (when used: simple line style)
- No stock photos of people unless extremely authentic

## UI Elements
- **Buttons:** Rounded corners (8px), solid fills, hover states with slight darkening
- **Forms:** Clean borders, ample padding, clear labels above fields
- **Cards:** Subtle shadows, cream backgrounds
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace, never cramped
