**Q: Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?**  
A: Absolutely. We work with vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and most allergy restrictions. Let us know during booking.

**Q: What if I need to cancel or reschedule?**  
A: 7+ days notice allows 50% refund of deposit. Less than 7 days notice = deposit is non-refundable. Rescheduling is free if done 14+ days in advance (subject to availability).

**Q: Do you provide serving staff?**  
A: Chef service includes chef preparation and plating. For events over 12 guests, we can coordinate additional serving staff (additional cost).

**Q: What if my location is outside your service area?**  
A: We can often accommodate with a travel fee. Contact us with your location for a custom quote.

**Q: Do you offer tastings before booking?**  
A: For events over $3,000, we can arrange a tasting (cost applied to final invoice if you book).

**Q: How do payments work?**  
A: 30-40% deposit via Stripe or bank transfer to confirm. Remaining balance due 3 days before event. We accept cards, ACH, or check.

**Q: Can you source specific ingredients?**  
A: Yes! If you have favorite local farms or specific requests, we'll work with them. We have established relationships with 15+ Vermont farms.
