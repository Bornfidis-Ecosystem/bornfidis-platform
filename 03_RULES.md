# BORNFIDIS BUSINESS RULES

## Booking & Scheduling Rules

### Lead Times:
- Private chef (4-8 guests): 14 days minimum
- Private chef (9-12 guests): 21 days minimum
- Events (13+ guests): 28 days minimum
- Holiday season events: 60 days minimum

### Service Radius:
- **No travel fee zone:** 20-mile radius from Ludlow, VT
- **Standard travel fee:** 21-40 miles = $75
- **Extended travel:** 41+ miles = custom quote

### Peak Days & Pricing:
- Friday/Saturday dinners in summer: standard pricing
- Holidays (Thanksgiving week, Christmas week, New Year's): +20% premium
- Last-minute bookings (under 7 days): +30% premium (if we can accommodate)

---

## Payment & Deposit Rules

### Deposits:
- **Private chef:** 30% non-refundable deposit
- **Corporate/large events:** 40% non-refundable deposit
- **Deposit methods:** Stripe (credit card), bank transfer, check

### Final Payment:
- Due 3 business days before event
- If not received, event may be cancelled without refund

### Refund Policy:
- **14+ days notice:** 50% refund of total paid
- **7-13 days notice:** 50% refund of deposit only
- **Less than 7 days:** No refund
- **Emergency cancellations:** Evaluated case-by-case

### Rescheduling:
- **14+ days notice:** Free reschedule (subject to availability)
- **7-13 days notice:** $100 rescheduling fee
- **Less than 7 days:** Not permitted without full rebooking

---

## Service Standards

### Communication Expectations:
- Initial inquiry response: within 24 hours (business days)
- Quote/proposal delivery: within 48 hours
- Menu finalization: 7 days before event
- Final headcount confirmation: 3 days before event

### What We Provide:
- All food ingredients (locally sourced when possible)
- Basic cooking equipment (we bring essentials)
- Serving platters and utensils
- Kitchen cleanup

### What Client Provides:
- Access to working kitchen (stove, oven, refrigerator)
- Dining table setup and chairs
- Dinnerware, glassware, flatware (or we can coordinate rental for fee)
- Beverages (unless included in package)

### Dietary Accommodations:
- Must be specified at booking
- Major changes after menu finalization: may incur additional cost
- Severe allergies: require detailed discussion during booking

---

## Farmer/Partner Rules (Future Phase - Island Harvest Hub)

### Supplier Requirements:
- Must meet quality standards (defined in farmer onboarding)
- Reliable delivery schedules
- Transparent pricing
- Food safety compliance

### Payment Terms to Farmers:
- 70% of sale price paid to farmer
- Payment within 7 days of sale
- Monthly statements provided

---

## Data Retention

### Customer Data:
- Booking information retained for 2 years
- Payment records retained for 7 years (tax compliance)
- Marketing opt-ins: retained until opt-out requested

### Deletion Requests:
- Honored within 30 days
- Some records retained for legal/tax compliance even after deletion request
