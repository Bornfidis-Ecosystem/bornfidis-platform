# Bornfidis Academy Knowledge Map

A curriculum blueprint that connects every guide, course, and future program under the Bornfidis mission. The map organizes knowledge into **four pillars**, each with learning paths and guides.

---

## Four pillars

### 1. Food Systems

*Regenerative agriculture and food enterprise*

**Core guides**

- Regenerative Farm System
- Small Farm Profit System
- Caribbean Spice Trade Guide
- Village Food Network Manual

**Skills taught:** soil health, crop planning, market selling, food supply chains.

**Powers:** Bornfidis Provisions, ProJu (Island Harvest Hub).

---

### 2. Clothing & Product Craft

*Apparel, branding, and physical product businesses*

**Core guides**

- Bornfidis Apparel Blueprint
- Craft Product Branding Guide
- Outdoor Apparel Design System
- Print-On-Demand Business Guide

**Skills taught:** product development, label design, brand building, e-commerce.

**Powers:** Bornfidis Sportswear.

---

### 3. Housing & Infrastructure

*Village development and regenerative building*

**Core guides**

- Village Economy Blueprint
- Regenerative Village Planning
- Container Housing Systems
- Community Infrastructure Guide

**Skills taught:** land planning, cooperative economics, village design, infrastructure.

**Powers:** Future Bornfidis housing division.

---

### 4. Education & Enterprise

*Entrepreneurship and knowledge systems*

**Core guides**

- Chef Entrepreneur Manual
- Bornfidis Business Framework
- Village Enterprise Systems
- Regenerative Business Strategy

**Skills taught:** financial thinking, systems design, business models, leadership.

**Powers:** Bornfidis Academy itself.

---

## Curriculum ladder

| Level | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **1** | Foundations | Core guides and principles |
| **2** | Practitioners | Applied systems and skills |
| **3** | Builders | Enterprise development |
| **4** | Village Builders | Community and infrastructure systems |

This ladder aligns with the **Village Builder** membership concept.

---

## Example learning path (Food track)

```
Regenerative Farm System
        ↓
Small Farm Profit System
        ↓
Village Food Network
        ↓
Village Economy Blueprint
```

This path teaches students how to move from **individual skill → community impact**.

---

## How to use this map

- **Organize future manuals** — Assign new guides to a pillar and a ladder level.
- **Structure courses** — Build courses from one or across pillars.
- **Explain the vision** — Use the four pillars and ladder with partners, investors, and students.
- **Show learning paths** — Surface recommended sequences (e.g. Food track above) in the Academy UI when products and taxonomy align to this map.
