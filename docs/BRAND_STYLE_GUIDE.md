### Responsive Typography

**Mobile (< 768px):**
- Reduce hero heading to 36px
- Reduce H1 to 30px
- Reduce H2 to 24px
- Keep body text at 16px minimum

**Tablet (768px - 1024px):**
- Use 90% of desktop sizes
- Maintain readability

**Desktop (> 1024px):**
- Use full type scale
- Maximum line length: 75 characters

- Stock photos of generic farms
- Overly polished/corporate imagery
- Cold color temperatures
- Staged "perfect" scenes
- Non-Jamaican contexts

**When to Use:**
- Icons for features
- Infographics for data
- Process diagrams
- Educational materials

**Style:**
- Line art with navy/gold accents
- Simple, clear, functional
- Consistent stroke weight (2-3px)
- Rounded corners (4px radius)

## üé≠ Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes

**Professional but Accessible**
- Use clear, direct language
- Avoid jargon unless explaining it
- Speak with authority earned through experience

**Faith-Grounded without Being Preachy**
- Reference biblical principles naturally
- Use phrases like "stewardship," "provision," "faithful work"
- Avoid heavy-handed evangelism

**Jamaica-Centric**
- Use Jamaican context in examples
- Reference Portland Parish specifically
- Include Patois phrases where appropriate (with translations)
- Celebrate Jamaican culture and resilience

**Systems-Focused, Not Motivational**
- Provide concrete steps, not inspiration
- Include templates, worksheets, frameworks
- "Here's how" not "You can do it!"
- Discipline over motivation

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Homepage Hero** | Bold, Direct | "Build Regenerative Enterprise in Jamaica - Start Here" |
| **Academy Sales** | Confident, Specific | "60-page operating manual. 12 ready-to-use templates. $39." |
| **Email Nurture** | Warm, Personal | "Mi know seh you busy, but this one template save you 5 hours..." |
| **Error Messages** | Helpful, Calm | "Something went wrong. Try refreshing, or contact support@bornfidis.com" |
| **Success Messages** | Affirming, Clear | "Purchase complete! Check your email for download link." |

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use active voice ("Build your business" not "Your business can be built")
- Write short sentences (15-20 words average)
- Use specific numbers ("60-page manual" not "comprehensive guide")
- Include concrete examples from Jamaica
- Reference biblical stewardship naturally

**DON'T:**
- Use corporate buzzwords ("synergy," "leverage," "paradigm")
- Make vague promises ("unlimited potential," "guaranteed success")
- Overuse exclamation marks
- Write in all caps (except acronyms)
- Use complex academic language

## üì± Digital Applications

### Website Design Principles

**Layout:**
- Clean, spacious (generous white space)
- Card-based components with subtle shadows
- Consistent padding (16px, 24px, 32px, 48px)
- Maximum content width: 1280px

**Navigation:**
- Logo left, menu right
- Sticky header on scroll
- Mobile: Hamburger menu with logo-only
- Clear hierarchy (Academy primary)

**Buttons:**
- Primary: Gold background, navy text, bold
- Secondary: Navy outline, transparent background
- Hover: Darken by 10%
- Border radius: 6px
- Padding: 12px 24px (medium), 16px 32px (large)

**Cards:**
- White background
- Border: 1px solid light gray
- Border radius: 8px
- Shadow: subtle (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08))
- Padding: 24px

### Responsive Breakpoints

```css
/* Mobile First */
mobile: 0px - 767px
tablet: 768px - 1023px
desktop: 1024px - 1439px
wide: 1440px+
```

## üìã Usage Examples

### Correct Logo Usage

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Use on solid backgrounds (navy, white, cream)
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Provide clear space
- Use high-resolution files
- Link logo to homepage

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Place on busy backgrounds
- Stretch or distort
- Change colors
- Add effects (shadows, gradients, glows)
- Rotate or skew

### Color Combinations

‚úÖ **Approved Combinations:**
- Navy background + Gold accent + White text
- White background + Navy text + Gold accent
- Cream background + Navy text + Forest green accent
- Gold background + Navy text (use sparingly)

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- Gold on white (low contrast)
- Navy on forest green (muddy)
- All gold everything (overwhelming)
- Multiple bright colors together
